## Problem Statement
In the time of technocracy , common people face many problems , especially the lack of knowledge about the technology.
So, we need a way to provide them with their voices being heard by the authorites.
That system should be simple to understand for the people and also, be strong enough for storage of information.
## Scope of the Project
The project has to;
- Input the required information from the user.
- Store them in the csv file.
- Commit changes according to the provided information.
- Have multiple functions in its code to perform different roles.
## Target Users
This project will be most valuable for the people who have no other means of sending their complaints to the nearby authorities.
The reason can be long distances, long working hours or in simple lack of understanding.
## Features
- The design of this project is kept simple for the users to understand simply.
- Deletion of all information is made impossible to prevent tampering of complaints.
- CSV file is imported in Python due to its great storage capability.
- Chances of error are low , unless input given is incorrect.
